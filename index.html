My interest in crocheting grew after spending the summer at my grandmas watching her knit sweaters for us.

The idea to start a crochet/knitting materials shop came to me in 2017as I was making a doll for my first client.

With the help of my parents and siblings we cleared the basement and set up a packaging/dispatch area in our basement and we have not looked back since then.

We are now what we are because of Loyal customers like you. That’s our history.
